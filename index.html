Featured Products

Scotch

Single Malt Scotch, Scotch Whiskey Pflugerville, Glenfiddich, Glenlivet, Macallan Scotch

Description: Scotch is a whisky made in Scotland, known for its distinctive smoky flavor. Top brands like Glenfiddich, Macallan, and Glenlivet bring elegance to every glass. Perfect for connoisseurs and collectors alike.

History: The production of Scotch dates back to the 15th century, with monks being the first known distillers in Scotland. Over the centuries, Scotch has developed into one of the most refined spirits in the world.

Whiskey

Bourbon Whiskey, Rye Whiskey, Jack Daniel's, Maker's Mark, Buffalo Trace Whiskey

Description: Whiskey is a versatile spirit with roots in both the U.S. and Ireland. Whether it's Bourbon from Kentucky or Rye from Canada, this classic drink is perfect for any occasion.

History: Whiskey distillation has been traced to medieval monks in Ireland and Scotland, but it flourished in the U.S. in the 18th century, creating the famed American Bourbon and Rye varieties.

Rum

Best Rum in Pflugerville, Captain Morgan, Bacardi, Dark Rum, White Rum

Description: Rum, crafted from sugarcane byproducts such as molasses, offers a rich, tropical flavor. Whether enjoyed in cocktails or straight, brands like Bacardi and Captain Morgan deliver a smooth taste.

History: Originating in the Caribbean in the 17th century, rum was initially produced on sugarcane plantations and became a key export for the colonial economy. It has since become a beloved spirit worldwide.

Beer

Craft Beer Pflugerville, IPA, Lager, Stout, Local Texas Beer, Budweiser, Shiner Bock

Description: Offering a vast selection of domestic and craft beers, including IPAs, Lagers, and Stouts, Happy Town Liquor caters to both casual drinkers and beer aficionados. Featuring local favorites like Shiner Bock.

History: Beer is one of the oldest beverages in human history, with roots tracing back to ancient Mesopotamia. It has evolved into a global favorite, offering endless styles and flavors.

Cocktail Recipes

Classic Margarita

Ingredients: 2 oz Tequila, 1 oz Lime juice, 1 oz Triple sec, Salt for rim

Instructions: Rim the glass with salt. Shake the ingredients with ice and strain into the glass.

Old Fashioned

Ingredients: 2 oz Bourbon, 1 sugar cube, 2 dashes Angostura bitters, Orange twist

Instructions: Muddle the sugar and bitters in a glass. Add bourbon and ice, stir, and garnish with an orange twist.

Mojito

Ingredients: 2 oz Rum, 1 oz Lime juice, 2 tsp Sugar, Mint leaves, Soda water

Instructions: Muddle mint and sugar in a glass. Add lime juice and rum, then fill with ice. Top with soda water.

Manhattan

Ingredients: 2 oz Rye whiskey, 1 oz Sweet vermouth, 2 dashes Angostura bitters, Maraschino cherry

Instructions: Stir ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled glass. Garnish with a cherry.

Moscow Mule

Ingredients: 2 oz Vodka, 1 oz Lime juice, 4 oz Ginger beer, Lime wedge

Instructions: Combine vodka and lime juice in a mug filled with ice. Top with ginger beer and garnish with a lime wedge.
